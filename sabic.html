Discover the SABIC Station's routes and connections on the Yellow and Purple Lines.


The SABIC Station lies at the intersection of the Yellow and Purple lines, making travel between Airport T1-2, An Naseem, and other key stops accessible.

To reach SABIC station from blue line, first, go to KAFD station, from there you can go on Yellow line or Purple line.

FAQs

What is the SABIC Station known for?

The SABIC Station is a state-of-the-art transportation hub that connects various parts of the city with modern facilities and seamless access to major destinations.


How do I get a discounted ticket?

Discounted tickets are available for students, seniors, and certain other groups. You can apply for a discount card through our website or at the station’s customer service counter.


What are the operating hours of SABIC Station?

The station operates daily from 5:00 AM to 12:00 AM.
